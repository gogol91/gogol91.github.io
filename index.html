About

Sayak Datta is an Assistant Professor at the Gran Sasso Science Institute (GSSI), Italy. He works in gravitational wave physics, focusing on compact binaries, black holes, neutron stars, and extreme mass ratio inspirals (EMRIs). His research involves modeling strong gravity systems and extracting physical information from gravitational wave signals.

Research Interests

  • Extreme mass ratio inspirals
  • Black hole physics
  • Neutron star physics
  • Gravitational waves
  • Compact binary coalescence
